Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

The site will contain detailed information about different tanks, including their history, specifications, images, and comparisons.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Content will be sourced from reliable military history archives, official tank manufacturer websites, and public domain images.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Tank enthusiasts, historians, and military fans will find valuable, well-organized, and visually engaging information about tanks in one place.

Why should a site be built for this?

There is a need for an interactive and well-structured online resource that presents tank information in an engaging way.

What would you like users to do at your site?

Users should explore different tanks, compare specifications, and learn about tank history in an engaging format.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal is to create an accessible, educational, and visually appealing resource for tank enthusiasts and researchers.
